Data represents 10 female participants frailty symptoms.The key indicator is grip strength measured using dynamometer.The Height of the participant is measured in inches, Weight in pounds, Age in years, The grip strength that is static force the participant can exert by squeezing a dynamometer measured in kilograms.Frailty is categorical attribute which indicates the presence or absence of the symptoms, "Y" denotes "Yes", participant has frailty symptoms and "N" denotes "No", participant does not have frailty symptoms.
